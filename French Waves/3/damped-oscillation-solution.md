## Problem Statement
Verify that $x = Ae^{-\alpha t} \cos \omega t$ is a possible solution of the equation

$$\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} + \gamma \frac{dx}{dt} + \omega_0^2x = 0$$

and find $\alpha$ and $\omega$ in terms of $\gamma$ and $\omega_0$.
