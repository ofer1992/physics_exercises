## Problem Statement
Find the frequency of the combined motion of each of the following:
(a) $\sin(2\pi t - \sqrt{2}) + \cos(2\pi t)$.
(b) $\sin(12\pi t) + \cos(13\pi t - \pi/4)$.
(c) $\sin(3t) - \cos(\pi t)$.
