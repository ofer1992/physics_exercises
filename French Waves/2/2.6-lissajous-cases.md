## Problem Statement
Construct the Lissajous figures for the following motions:
(a) $x = \cos 2\omega t$, $y = \sin 2\omega t$.
(b) $x = \cos 2\omega t$, $y = \cos(2\omega t - \pi/4)$.
(c) $x = \cos 2\omega t$, $y = \cos \omega t$.
