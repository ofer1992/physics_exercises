## Problem Statement
If $B$ is uniform, show that $A(r) = -\frac{1}{2}(r \times B)$ works. That is, check that $\nabla \cdot A = 0$ and $\nabla \times A = B$. Is this result unique, or are there other functions with the same divergence and curl?
