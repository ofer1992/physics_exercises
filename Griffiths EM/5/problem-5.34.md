## Problem Statement
Show that the magnetic field of a dipole can be written in coordinate-free form:

$$B_{dip}(r) = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi}\frac{1}{r^3}[3(m \cdot \hat{r})\hat{r} - m]$$
