## Problem Statement
A certain current distribution produces the magnetic field

$$B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi}\frac{1}{s^3}(z\hat{s} + s\hat{z})$$

where $s$ is the distance from the z axis. Find the vector potential $A$.
