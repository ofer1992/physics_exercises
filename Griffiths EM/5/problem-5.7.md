## Problem Statement
For a configuration of charges and currents confined within a volume $V$, show that

$$\int_V J\,d\tau = \frac{dp}{dt}$$

where $p$ is the total dipole moment. [Hint: evaluate $\int_V \nabla \cdot (xJ)\,d\tau$.]
